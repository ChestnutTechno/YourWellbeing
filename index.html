Want To Measure Your Stress Levels?

You may like to use the questionnaire below to measure your current stress levels. The Stress Symptoms Checklist lists 52 physical and mental symptoms of stress. Check all the symptoms you have felt over the past month and then count up your total score. You can then check your stress levels using the table at the bottom.
