Mental Health

Mental health of international student

International students encounter a variety of mental illness in Australia.

This videos highlight some of the symptoms and behaviours people may experience due to changes to their situation, stress or isolation, and identify tips and resources available that can provide much needed support.




Stress level testing

Want to measure your stress levels?

You may like to measure your current stress levels. The Stress Symptoms Checklist lists 52 physical and mental symptoms of stress. Check all the symptoms you have felt over the past month and then count up your total score. You can then check your stress levels using the table at the bottom.

Physical Symptoms


  Headaches(migraine or tension)
  Backaches
  Tight muscles
  Neck and shoulder pain
  Jaw tension
  Muscle cramps, spasms
  Nervous stomach
  Nausea
  Other pain

Psychological Symptoms


  Anxiety
  Depression
  Confusion or spaciness
  Irrational fears
  Compulsive behaviors
  Forgetfulness
  Feeling overloaded or overwhelmed
  Mood swings
